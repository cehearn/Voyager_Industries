Ship Specifications

Voyager Space Industries is known for carrying the highest quality liner ship and is the recipient of the Steve Robinson Award in ship design for 23 consecutive years. Constructed in 3014, the Magellan class boasts top-of-the-line Eurellium alloy construct that's built to last while still representing all things fine flying. However, don't be fooled by its elegant appearance. The Magellan has 5 armaments: 3 Hoffer & Rhine Beam Lasers and 2 Smith Lever Rail Guns. Your safety is gauranteed so you could enjoy the comfort of your galactic travels.
